Florian Burkhardt

Full-Stack Developer and Designer for both Web and Desktop environments.

I enjoy designing and building software with full control over structure, behavior, and user experience. I care about clean architecture, focused problem-solving, and applications that feel direct and responsive.

I strongly believe in minimalism. Modern software often suffers from feature bloat. I prefer tools that do exactly what they're meant to do. Nothing more, nothing less. This idea is what led me to found my own company and publish refined versions of the tools I originally built for myself.

While I enjoy working across the full stack, I especially like seeing things come to life in the UI, even though most of my strengths lie in backend architecture and systems design.

Aside from public releases, I also work on a lot of stuff in private.
For example, small Python utilities, experimental desktop applications with Electron and Tauri, or currently a custom desktop environment for my Arch Linux installation using Quickshell and Hyprland.
